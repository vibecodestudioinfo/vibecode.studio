Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about mobile app development with us. Can't find the answer? Contact our team →

Native vs. Cross-Platform: What should I choose?
For 95% of startups, cross-platform (using React Native or Flutter) is the optimal path. It radically reduces time-to-market and development costs by 30-40% while delivering a near-native experience. If your app relies heavily on proprietary hardware APIs, complex AR features, or requires absolute maximum performance, we will guide you toward purely native development (Swift/Kotlin).
Do you handle App Store and Google Play submissions?
Absolutely! Dealing with Apple and Google review guidelines is notorious. Our end-to-end service encompasses everything from certificates and provisioning profiles to final, approved deployment in the app stores. We handle the entire submission process, including creating store listings, screenshots, and responding to review feedback, ensuring a smooth launch.
How much does it cost to build a mobile app?
Costs vary depending on backend complexity, feature set, design requirements, and platform choice. Starter MVPs typically range from ₹2,00,000 to ₹5,00,000 for cross-platform apps, while feature-rich consumer apps with complex backend infrastructure can range from ₹6,00,000 to ₹15,00,000+. We provide a personalized, transparent quote after understanding your specific requirements during a free consultation.
How long does it take to develop a mobile MVP?
A typical mobile MVP takes between 8 to 14 weeks depending on complexity and feature scope. For simple utility apps with basic authentication and core functionality, we can deliver in 6-8 weeks. More complex apps with social features, real-time sync, or marketplace functionality typically require 10-14 weeks. We focus on rapid iteration to get your product in users' hands quickly.
Do you provide post-launch maintenance and updates?
Yes! We offer flexible maintenance and support packages to ensure your app stays up-to-date with OS updates, security patches, and new feature rollouts. Our post-launch services include bug fixes, performance monitoring, analytics integration, and regular feature updates. We also provide emergency support for critical issues with guaranteed response times.
How do you ensure app security and data protection?
Security is our top priority. We implement industry best practices including encrypted data storage, secure API authentication (OAuth2, JWT), HTTPS/TLS encryption, and regular security audits. We also ensure compliance with data protection regulations like GDPR and follow Apple and Google's security guidelines. All source code and data remain your intellectual property.
