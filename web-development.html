Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us. Can't find the answer? Contact our team →

How long does it take to develop a web MVP?
Typically, a robust web app MVP takes between 4 to 8 weeks depending on complexity. We focus on engineering the core functionalities flawlessly to get your product to market as fast as possible. For standard MVPs, we offer a 4-week sprint option with essential features ready for user testing and investor demos.
What technologies do you use for web development?
Our primary tech stack includes React, Next.js, Node.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. We choose modern tools that guarantee scalability, blazing fast load times, and superior SEO performance. For backend, we work with Node.js, Python, and cloud platforms like AWS, Vercel, and Firebase to ensure your application is production-ready from day one.
Do you assist with post-launch maintenance?
Yes! We offer ongoing retainer and maintenance packages to ensure your web application stays secure, updated, and scales seamlessly as your user base grows. Our maintenance plans include security patches, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and feature enhancements. We also provide 24/7 emergency support for critical issues.
What is your pricing model?
We offer flexible pricing based on your project scope and requirements. For startups, we typically work with fixed-price project-based models for MVPs, or hourly retainers for ongoing development. Most MVP projects range from ₹1,50,000 to ₹5,00,000 depending on complexity. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs during a free consultation call.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Absolutely! Early-stage startups and first-time founders are our specialty. We understand the constraints of bootstrapped teams and offer flexible payment structures, MVP-focused development to validate ideas quickly, and strategic guidance on technical decisions that won't lead to costly reworks. Over 70% of our clients are pre-seed or seed-stage startups.
Can you redesign or migrate my existing website?
Yes, we specialize in website redesigns and migrations. Whether you're moving from a legacy system to a modern stack, need a design refresh, or want to improve performance and SEO, we handle the entire migration process seamlessly with zero downtime. We ensure all content, SEO rankings, and user data are preserved during the transition.
