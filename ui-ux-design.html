Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our UI/UX design process. Can't find the answer? Contact our team →

What tools do you use for UI/UX Design?
Figma is our primary workspace. It allows for real-time collaboration with your team, interactive prototyping, and seamless handoffs customized perfectly for modern frontend developers. We also use Miro for user journey mapping, user flows, and collaborative workshops to ensure everyone is aligned throughout the design process.
Can you redesign our existing application?
Yes. We start with a comprehensive UX audit of your current platform to identify drop-off points and friction, then redesign the workflow iteratively to ensure maximum ROI without totally alienating your current active user base. We involve your users in the research phase through surveys and usability testing to ensure the new design truly addresses their pain points.
Do you also develop the designs you create?
Yes! While we can easily hand off Figma files to your internal developers, we are a full-service technical agency. We highly recommend having our web and mobile engineering teams build the designs for 1:1 pixel perfection. Our design and development teams work in sync, ensuring that the final product matches the prototype exactly with no visual discrepancies or UX gaps.
How long does the design process take?
Timeline depends on project complexity. A typical MVP design (10-15 screens) takes 2-4 weeks, including research, wireframing, UI design, and interactive prototyping. Complex SaaS platforms or full product redesigns typically take 4-8 weeks. We work in agile sprints, providing regular checkpoints for feedback and iteration to keep the project moving efficiently.
Do you involve user testing in your process?
Absolutely! User testing is a critical part of our design methodology. We conduct moderated and unmoderated usability tests on prototypes with real users to validate our design decisions before development begins. This approach helps us catch usability issues early, saving significant time and cost on post-launch fixes.
Do you provide design systems and style guides?
Yes, we deliver comprehensive design systems and component libraries alongside your final designs. This includes typography scales, color systems, UI component documentation, and interaction patterns. This ensures consistency across your product and makes future feature development faster and more cohesive.
