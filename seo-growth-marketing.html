Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our growth marketing services. Can't find the answer? Contact our team →

How long does it take to see results from SEO?
SEO is a leading indicator game. Generally, you will see keyword traction and initial ranking improvements within 2-3 months, with significant compounding lead volume starting around the 6-month mark. It is a long-term investment that yields the highest ROI of any channel. We provide monthly reporting so you can track progress every step of the way.
Can you help with B2B SaaS SEO specifically?
Yes. B2B SaaS relies heavily on lower-volume, hyper-targeted search queries (e.g., "alternatives to [Competitor]" or "[Use Case] software"). We specialize in mapping content to the exact stages of the enterprise buyer's journey—from awareness to consideration to decision—helping you capture qualified leads ready to convert.
Do you use AI for content generation?
We securely use AI for data clustering, outline generation, and programmatic structures. However, core editorial content is always reviewed and infused with human, expert-level insight to ensure it meets Google's strict E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) guidelines. We believe AI is a tool, not a replacement for genuine expertise.
What metrics do you track and report on?
We provide comprehensive monthly reports covering organic traffic growth, keyword rankings (top 3, top 10, top 20), click-through rates, conversion rates, backlink acquisition, and most importantly—leads generated and customer acquisition cost. We focus on metrics that directly impact your bottom line, not vanity metrics.
Do you offer link building services?
Yes, we offer white-hat link building through digital PR, guest posting on reputable industry publications, resource page outreach, and broken link building. We never buy links or participate in spammy practices that could harm your domain authority. Quality over quantity is our approach to building a strong backlink profile.
Do you work with local businesses or only SaaS startups?
While our specialty is B2B SaaS and tech startups, we also work with local businesses looking to dominate their geographic market. Our local SEO services include Google Business Profile optimization, local citation building, and hyper-local content strategies to help you appear in the map pack and capture customers in your area.
