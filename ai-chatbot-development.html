Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI chatbot development. Can't find the answer? Contact our team →

Will the AI hallucinate or give wrong answers?
To entirely prevent hallucinations, we use a sophisticated architecture called Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). The AI is stringently restricted to only answer questions using the isolated, verified data provided within your company's internal knowledge base, documentation, and FAQs. This ensures responses are accurate, consistent, and aligned with your brand voice. We also implement confidence thresholds to gracefully handle questions outside its knowledge scope.
What models do you actually use?
While we frequently utilize industry leaders like OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4 Turbo) and Anthropic (Claude 3.5 Sonnet) for their superior reasoning architecture and multi-modal capabilities, we can also deploy secure, self-hosted open-source models (like Llama 3, Mistral, or Mixtral) for highly sensitive data requirements or cost optimization. We help you choose the right model based on your specific use case, data privacy needs, and budget.
Can the chatbot connect out to other apps?
Yes. With robust API integration, the chatbot isn't exclusively conversational—it acts as an agent. It can easily query inventory levels, update an active CRM file (HubSpot, Salesforce), process a real-time refund via Stripe, create support tickets in Zendesk, schedule calendar events, send Slack notifications, or even trigger Zapier workflows. We build custom actions that make your chatbot a true automation partner.
How do you handle data privacy and security?
Data security is our top priority. We implement enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, and compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and SOC2 standards. For sensitive data, we can deploy chatbots on your own infrastructure (VPC/on-premise) with self-hosted LLMs, ensuring your customer data never leaves your environment. All conversations are encrypted and can be configured with automatic data retention policies.
Can the chatbot speak multiple languages?
Absolutely. Our AI chatbots support 50+ languages out of the box. The model automatically detects the user's language and responds in the same language. This is perfect for startups with international customers. We can also fine-tune the model for specific regional dialects or industry-specific terminology to ensure natural, localized conversations.
How do you measure chatbot performance?
We provide comprehensive analytics dashboards tracking key metrics including: resolution rate (percentage of conversations fully handled by AI), customer satisfaction scores (CSAT), average response time, conversation handoff rate to humans, frequently asked questions, and cost savings calculations. We use these insights to continuously fine-tune your AI agent, improve its accuracy, and identify gaps in your knowledge base.
