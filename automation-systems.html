Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about business automation. Can't find the answer? Contact our team →

Do you use tools like Zapier/Make or write custom code?
We use both. For straightforward, rapid deployments, platforms like Make.com or n8n are fantastic. However, when you hit logical limitations, need complex branching, or face astronomical API volume costs, we engineer hyper-efficient bespoke microservices using Node.js and Python to handle the precise routing natively. We always recommend the most cost-effective and maintainable solution for your specific use case.
What processes can be automated?
Nearly any digital process that follows a logical ruleset. The most common targets are CRM syncing (HubSpot, Salesforce), financial reporting and reconciliation, document processing (invoices, contracts), employee onboarding flows, database migrations, inter-departmental notifications, lead scoring, and customer support ticket routing. If it's repetitive and rule-based, we can automate it.
Is my data secure during these automated transfers?
Security is foundational. We ensure that API keys, OAuth tokens, and sensitive payloads are heavily encrypted and strictly handled via secure environments natively or through compliant integration platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure. All data transfers use TLS 1.3 encryption, and we implement least-privilege access controls. We can also ensure compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, or SOC2 requirements.
How long does it take to implement an automation system?
Timeline depends on complexity. Simple Zapier/API integrations can be implemented in 1-2 weeks. Complex custom automation systems with custom internal dashboards and multi-step workflows typically take 4-8 weeks. We work in agile sprints, providing weekly demos and incremental value delivery so you start seeing time savings immediately.
What kind of ROI can I expect from automation?
Most clients see full ROI within 3-6 months. We typically calculate ROI based on hours saved × hourly cost of manual work. A typical automation project saves 15-25 hours per week for your team, which translates to ₹1,50,000 - ₹3,00,000+ in monthly savings. Beyond financial ROI, automation also reduces error rates, improves data accuracy, and enables faster response times to customers.
Do you provide ongoing support for automation systems?
Yes! We offer maintenance and support packages for all automation systems we build. This includes monitoring for failures, updating API integrations when third-party tools change, scaling infrastructure as your volume grows, and adding new features as your business evolves. We also provide documentation and training so your team understands how to manage and extend the automation.
